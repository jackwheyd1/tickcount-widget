About the App

TickCount Widget is an offline countdown timer for iPhone. Track important dates like exams, birthdays, anniversaries, project deadlines, and trips — all stored locally on your device.

Countdown Dashboard

See your most important countdowns at a glance with large, clear day counters.

Calendar View

Browse your countdowns in a monthly calendar with color-coded event dots.

Export & Share

Generate beautiful PNG images or PDF documents of any countdown to share.

Local Reminders

Set per-event reminder notifications — processed entirely on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. TickCount Widget works completely offline. There are no accounts, no syncing, and no network requests.

Is my data backed up?

In the current version, countdown data is stored locally on your device. A backup/restore feature is planned for a future update.

Can I use the app on iPad or Mac?

Currently TickCount Widget is designed for iPhone only (iOS 17.0+). iPad and Mac support may be added in the future.

Are there in-app purchases or ads?

No. TickCount Widget has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

How do I delete an event?

Swipe left on any event in the event list, or open the event and tap the delete button in the editor.
