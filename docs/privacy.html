Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

TL;DR — TickCount Widget collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

1. Data We Collect

We don't collect anything. TickCount Widget is a fully offline countdown timer app. All your events, dates, notes, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone and never transmitted anywhere.

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics or crash reporting SDKs
  • No advertising or marketing trackers
  • No third-party services or APIs

2. Local Notifications

If you enable local reminders, the app schedules notifications using Apple's UserNotifications framework. These notifications are processed entirely on your device by iOS. No reminder data is sent to any server.

3. Export & Share

When you export a countdown as PNG or PDF, the file is written to a temporary local directory and presented via the iOS system share sheet. You choose where to share it — the app itself does not access or transmit the exported file.

4. Children's Privacy

Since the app collects no personal information from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages.

5. Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store any of your data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete. Your data lives on your device and is under your control at all times.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page. Since the app requires no network access, you will not receive in-app notifications of policy changes.
